Responsibility as a Marketing director


As a member of the event planning team at my organization, I have had the opportunity to take on the role of designing course materials for our technical workshops. This has included creating pamphlets, brochures, and other event materials for our guests.

In order to successfully execute this task, I have had to take on a number of responsibilities. First and foremost, I have been responsible for designing and developing the materials that we use at our workshops. This has involved choosing appropriate fonts, colors, and graphics to effectively convey the information that we want to impart to our guests. I have also had to ensure that all of the materials are properly formatted and easy to read, as we want our guests to have a positive experience at our workshops.

In addition to designing and developing the materials, I have also been responsible for photocopying them for distribution to our guests. This has required careful attention to detail, as we want to ensure that all of the materials are of high quality and that there are enough copies for everyone in attendance.

Another important aspect of my role has been connecting with potential sponsors for our events and workshops. This has involved reaching out to local businesses and organizations to see if they would be interested in supporting our efforts. I have also been responsible for researching interesting advertising trends on social media, as we want to make sure that we are using the most effective methods to promote our workshops.

Finally, I have also been responsible for monitoring our social media handles and using Google Analytics to track the success of our advertising efforts. By keeping a close eye on these metrics, we have been able to identify what works and what doesn't, and adjust our strategy accordingly.

Overall, designing course materials for our technical workshops has been a challenging but rewarding experience. It has given me the opportunity to develop my skills in design and event planning, and has helped me to become a valuable member of the team.
